Privacy Policy

At GabayGinhawa, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Wellness Tracker WebApp. By accessing or using GabayGinhawa, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


Information We Collect

a. Personal Information
When you sign up for GabayGinhawa, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, age, gender, height, weight, and any other information you provide voluntarily.

b. Health Information
We collect health-related information that you input into the application, including but not limited to your sleep patterns, caloric intake, physical activity, mood, water intake, and health goals.

c. Usage Data
We may collect information about how you interact with GabayGinhawa, including your browsing history, search queries, and interactions with features and functionalities.


How We Use Your Information

a. Personalization
We use the information collected to personalize your experience on GabayGinhawa, providing tailored health recommendations, tracking progress towards health goals, and offering insights to improve your well-being.

b. Communication
We may use your contact information to communicate with you about updates, new features, promotions, and other relevant information related to GabayGinhawa.

c. Analytics
We use usage data and aggregated information to analyze user behavior, improve our services, and enhance the user experience.


Information Sharing

a. Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating GabayGinhawa, such as hosting services, analytics providers, and customer support services.

b. Legal Compliance
We may disclose your information in response to legal processes, court orders, or government requests, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.


Data Security

a. We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

b. Despite our efforts to secure your information, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.


Children's Privacy

GabayGinhawa is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.


Changes to this Private Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be effective immediately upon posting the updated version on GabayGinhawa. Your continued use of GabayGinhawa after the posting of the updated Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.


Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or GabayGinhawa's privacy practices, please contact us at gabayginhawa@support.com.
